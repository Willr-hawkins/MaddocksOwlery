Welcome to MaddocksOwlery.

| A sanctuary for wildlife, a legacy for future generations. |

In the heart of North Devon, nestled among 35 acres of rich, diverse land, a quiet transformation is taking place. After more than three decades running local riding stables, Simon Maddocks turned a lifelong connection to the land into a powerful mission of nature recovery.


With the support of the North Devon National Landscape (formerly AONB), local stonemasons, conservation groups, and generous crowdfunding, a dream has come to life: a purpose-built wildlife tower, created to offer shelter to the creatures losing their habitats to development and disrepair.


This is more than just an owlery. It’s a long-term commitment to biodiversity. Carefully constructed with locally sourced materials and guided by a passion for the natural world, the tower stands as a symbol of what’s possible when community and conservation work hand-in-hand.


Cameras installed inside the tower allow us to observe barn owls and bats without disturbance. The upcoming viewing platform will offer safe and respectful access for anyone curious to witness nature in action—without harming the delicate balance of wildlife.


Please join us in celebrating and protecting the wildlife of North Devon—because every nest, every wingbeat, every wild corner matters.
